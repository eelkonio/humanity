# Digital Age Problems: Social Media, Attention Economy, and Digital Dysfunction

## Overview

Digital technologies promised connection, information, and empowerment. Instead, they often produce addiction, polarization, surveillance, manipulation, and disconnection. Understanding these problems reveals how technology exploits human psychology for profit.

## Major Problems

### Social Media Dysfunction

- **Addiction**: Dopamine manipulation, infinite scroll
- **Polarization**: Echo chambers, outrage algorithms
- **Mental health**: Anxiety, depression, body image
- **Misinformation**: Viral falsehoods
- **Harassment**: Cyberbullying, mob behavior

### Attention Economy

- **Distraction**: Constant interruption
- **Shallow processing**: Reduced deep thinking
- **Anxiety**: FOMO, notification stress
- **Productivity loss**: Multitasking costs
- **Relationship damage**: Phubbing, presence erosion

### Surveillance Capitalism

- **Privacy loss**: Constant tracking
- **Manipulation**: Behavioral prediction and control
- **Autonomy erosion**: Algorithmic nudging
- **Power concentration**: Data monopolies

## Mechanisms

### Exploitation of Psychology

- **Variable rewards**: Slot machine dynamics
- **Social validation**: Likes and shares
- **FOMO**: Fear of missing out
- **Outrage**: Emotional engagement
- **Tribalism**: In-group/out-group dynamics
